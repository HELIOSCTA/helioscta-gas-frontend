Price vs Physical Correlation — Daily Changes

Daily changes in price track daily changes in throughput, but with leverage. On trade date 2/26, AGT cash fell $1.91 (-31%) while Stony Point fell only 183 MDth (-10%). The price-to-flow beta is roughly 3:1 — a 10% physical loosening triggers ~30% price decline. This convexity exists because AGT runs near capacity: the marginal molecule's price is extremely sensitive to the supply-demand balance when the system is tight, and collapses rapidly once the constraint eases.
